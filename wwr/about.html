About Us

At Whitewater Rafting, our purpose is to empower our patrons with the opportunities and abilities to experiance the joys of Whitewater Rafting. History

Whitewater Rafting was founded in 2023 and is one of the best rafting agencies in the State of Idaho. It was founded for the sole purpose of bringing together adventurers and creating lasting memories.
