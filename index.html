Corbin Meacham | WDD 130

Hello! My name is Corbin Meacham and I am from Idaho. I enjoy a lot of outdoor activities, such as motorcycle riding and camping, as well as spending time with my family. I am also an avid hunter and fisherman and have enjoyed those things since I was very young. I grew up in Boise, Idaho, USA, and did not move away until I attended my undergrad courses at ISU in Pocatello, Idaho. After two years studying there, I got accepted to the pharmacy program at the Meridian Campus for ISU and moved back home to attend school from there. I completed my first year there, and then was diagnosed with a brain tumor. So, I put in for a medical leave while I prepped for doctor visits and decided, in the meantime, to take some online classes through BYU-I pathways. I am currently looking into some sort of coding or programming career as a fallback plan. Just in case I cannot return to pharmacy school in the future. So far, I am quite enjoying these classes and have learned so much in the past three weeks. I am starting to feel like I might be a grandmaster at coding by the time these seven weeks are up. I look forward to learning more along side each of you as this semester progresses.
