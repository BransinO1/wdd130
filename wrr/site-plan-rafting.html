Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this webpage is to establish and online presence for the marketing and promotion of our business. This will attract a larger audience of adventure seekers and whitewater rafting enthusiasts. This webpage will help to showcase the special rafting experiences that we provide and also make available the information that river rafters so desperately seek.

Audience

Who might our target audience here at Whitewater Rafting be? Well, it is a population of adventure seekers and outdoor enthusiasts who want to experience the wonderful and amazing experiences found in whitewater rafting. Is this activity available for groups or just individuals? This is an activity designed for both individuals and groups that want to get out into this exciting recreational activity. Where can more information be found at? We host plenty of information on this webpage here, but we also have flyers and experienced staff at our office which can help to answer even more of your questions.
